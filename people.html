Collaborators and Partners

The QAA Collaborative Enhancement Project involves partnerships across institutions addressing a range of assessment design challenges. Partner institutions contribute to the development of a modular taxonomy, combining project instructions with context-specific knowledge bases, that can be adapted for specific assessment design scenarios.

Partner institution details and collaborator profiles will be added as Phase 2 partnerships are formalised.
